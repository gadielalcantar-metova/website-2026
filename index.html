How much does custom AI development cost?

+

Custom AI development typically ranges from $50,000 to $500,000+, depending on complexity, data requirements, model training needs, and integration scope. Metova provides detailed estimates after a free discovery session where we assess your specific needs, existing infrastructure, and desired outcomes. Simple LLM integrations start around $25,000, while complex ML platforms with custom model training typically fall in the $150,000-$400,000 range.

What industries does Metova serve?

+

Metova serves 10+ industries including healthcare, fintech, fitness, entertainment, logistics, energy, retail, and enterprise SaaS. Our clients range from Fortune 500 companies like SiriusXM and TruGreen to high-growth startups like FitOn and MyBambu. We bring deep domain expertise to each vertical, understanding the unique regulatory, technical, and user experience challenges that each industry presents.

How long does it take to build an AI product?

+

A typical AI product takes 3 to 9 months from discovery to launch, depending on scope. Simple LLM integrations can ship in 4-6 weeks. Complex ML pipelines with custom model training may take 6-12 months. Metova's agile process delivers working prototypes within the first 2-4 weeks, so you see tangible progress from day one rather than waiting months for a big-bang reveal.

What's the difference between AI consulting and AI development?

+

AI consulting focuses on strategy, feasibility studies, and roadmapping — helping you decide what to build and how. AI development is the actual engineering: building, training, deploying, and maintaining AI systems. Metova offers both, providing end-to-end service from strategy through production deployment. Many clients start with a consulting engagement to validate feasibility before committing to full development.

Does Metova offer nearshore development teams?

+

Yes. Metova operates a nearshore development center in Guadalajara, Mexico, providing dedicated engineering teams in US time zones at competitive rates. Our Talent Solutions program offers Employer of Record services, making it easy to scale your team with vetted Latin American engineers who work as embedded members of your organization — same standup, same Slack, same sprint.

What AI technologies does Metova work with?

+

Metova works with leading AI technologies including OpenAI GPT, Claude (Anthropic), LangChain, TensorFlow, PyTorch, Hugging Face, computer vision frameworks like OpenCV, and vector databases like Pinecone and Weaviate. We specialize in LLM integration, natural language processing, predictive analytics, recommendation engines, and AI-powered SaaS platforms. We're technology-agnostic — we choose the best tool for each project.
