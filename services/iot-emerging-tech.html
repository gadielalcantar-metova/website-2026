Built to survive

not just a demo.

IoT products fail silently. A bug in a web app gets a crash report. A bug in a connected device gets recalled. We build with production edge cases in mind: spotty connectivity, battery constraints, hardware variance, and regulatory compliance.

Security is designed in from the start — not bolted on at the end. That means device certificate provisioning, encrypted OTA updates, secure boot chains, and zero-trust architecture between devices and cloud. Because when your product is deployed in the field, there's no hot reload.

FAQ

Frequently asked
questions.

What types of connected devices do you build for?

+

We've worked across consumer IoT (wearables, smart home), industrial and enterprise IoT (asset tracking, facility monitoring), fleet and logistics (vehicles, trailers, equipment), and healthcare devices. The common thread: products where software has to talk to hardware reliably, in the real world.

Do you handle hardware design or just software?

+

We're primarily a software company. We write firmware, build embedded software, and engineer the cloud infrastructure that talks to your devices. For hardware design and manufacturing, we work alongside your hardware partners or recommended vendors — we don't manufacture devices ourselves, but we know how to integrate with the teams who do.

What connectivity protocols and standards do you support?

+

We're protocol-agnostic and have worked across MQTT, BLE, Wi-Fi, LTE/5G, LoRaWAN, Zigbee, Thread, and CAN bus. We'll recommend the right stack for your power budget, range requirements, and data volume — not whatever we've most recently used.

How do you handle security for IoT products?

+

Security is designed in from the start — not bolted on at the end. That means device certificate provisioning, encrypted OTA updates, secure boot chains, mutual TLS for device-to-cloud communication, and role-based access on the dashboard layer. We follow OWASP IoT guidelines and can assist with regulatory requirements if your product needs them.

Can you integrate with our existing cloud infrastructure?

+

Yes. We've built on AWS IoT Core, Azure IoT Hub, and Google Cloud IoT, as well as custom MQTT brokers. If you already have infrastructure in place, we'll extend it. If you're starting fresh, we'll recommend the platform that fits your scale and cost profile.
