FAQ

Frequently asked
questions.

What kinds of AI products does Metova build?

+

We build LLM-powered applications, ML-driven platforms, AI-native SaaS tools, intelligent automation systems, and data pipelines. Whether you're integrating AI into an existing product or building an AI-first platform from scratch, we handle the full stack.

Do you work with OpenAI, Anthropic, and other LLM providers?

+

Yes. We've worked across the major LLM providers — OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, and open-source models via Ollama and HuggingFace. We recommend the right model for your use case based on performance, cost, and latency requirements — not brand loyalty.

How do you handle AI reliability and hallucination issues?

+

Production AI requires guardrails, evaluation pipelines, and fallback logic. We build structured output validation, retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) where appropriate, and continuous monitoring systems that catch model drift and quality degradation before users do.

Can you improve or extend an AI product we've already started?

+

Yes. We regularly take over AI products mid-build or help teams scale beyond their initial prototype. We'll audit what exists, identify where the architecture breaks down at scale, and build forward from there — rather than starting over unnecessarily.

What does a typical AI product engagement look like?

+

Most engagements start with a scoping sprint — defining the AI use case, evaluating model options, and mapping the data architecture. From there we move into iterative development, with working software shipped in the first two weeks. We avoid month-long planning phases and ship early.
