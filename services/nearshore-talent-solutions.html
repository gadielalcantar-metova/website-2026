FAQ

Frequently asked
questions.

Where are your nearshore teams located?

+

Our nearshore talent operates primarily from Guadalajara, Mexico — our Latin America headquarters. All team members work in US time zones, which means real overlap with your team during the full workday, not just a few hours in the morning.

How are engineers vetted before placement?

+

Every engineer goes through a multi-stage process: technical screening, live coding assessments, communication evaluations, and reference checks. We only place engineers we'd put on our own projects — because in many cases, we already have.

What's the difference between staff augmentation and a dedicated team?

+

Staff augmentation means adding individual contributors to your existing team — they work within your structure, your tools, your processes. A dedicated team is a full squad (engineers, QA, sometimes design and PM) that operates as its own unit, managed by Metova, working exclusively on your product. We offer both models depending on what you need.

How quickly can a team be up and running?

+

For staff augmentation, we can typically place engineers within 1–2 weeks. For a dedicated team, expect 2–4 weeks to assemble, onboard, and start shipping — depending on the size and specialization required. We don't rush placements; the right fit matters more than the fastest start.

Do we manage the team directly or does Metova?

+

Your choice. In staff augmentation, your team leads manage day-to-day work directly. In a dedicated team model, Metova provides engineering leadership and you set product direction. Many clients start with one model and evolve into the other as the engagement grows.
