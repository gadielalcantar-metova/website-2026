FAQ

Frequently asked
questions.

What's the difference between UX and UI design?

+

UX design focuses on how a product works — the structure, flows, and usability. UI design focuses on how it looks — the visual layer, typography, color, and components. At Metova we handle both together, because you can't ship a great interface by treating them as separate workstreams.

Who is this service best suited for?

+

Our UX/UI design work is built for startups, SaaS companies, and scaling product teams that need thoughtful product design without the overhead of a full internal design department. We embed closely and move at product speed.

When should a company invest in UX design?

+

UX design is valuable at every stage, but it's especially critical when launching a new product, redesigning an existing one, or when your analytics show users dropping off or not converting. The earlier you invest, the cheaper it is to fix what's broken.

Can you work with our existing engineering team?

+

Yes. We work as an extension of your team — alongside your engineers, product managers, and stakeholders. We produce annotated Figma files, component specs, and handoff documentation that eliminate implementation guesswork.

Do you design for web apps, mobile apps, or both?

+

Both. UX and UI design can be scoped for web platforms, SaaS dashboards, iOS apps, Android apps, and responsive interfaces — whatever your product needs. Many of our engagements span multiple platforms within the same project.
