FAQ

Frequently asked
questions.

What kinds of computer vision problems do you take on?

+

Object detection, segmentation, classification, OCR, pose estimation, motion tracking, anomaly detection, and video analytics. We handle both off-the-shelf model integration and custom training for domain-specific tasks that public models can't solve.

What if we don't have labeled training data?

+

We build the labeling pipeline. We set up annotation workflows, bring in labeled-data vendors when volume demands it, and use synthetic data generation and active learning to get to a trainable dataset faster than starting from scratch.

Can the model run on edge devices or offline?

+

Yes. We target cloud inference when latency and cost allow it, and edge deployment (NVIDIA Jetson, mobile, custom hardware) when you need real-time processing, offline capability, or strict data residency. We tune models with quantization, pruning, and ONNX/TensorRT for the target runtime.

How accurate will the model be?

+

Depends on your data and your tolerance. We set accuracy targets upfront based on the downstream decision — a safety-critical application has a different bar than content tagging. We benchmark against industry baselines, measure on your holdout set, and iterate until it clears the bar or we know why it can't.

What about ongoing model maintenance and retraining?

+

We treat models as software that degrades. We set up drift monitoring, retraining pipelines, and evaluation gates so models stay accurate as your data shifts. We document the full training lineage so your team can operate the system after handoff, not just inherit it.
