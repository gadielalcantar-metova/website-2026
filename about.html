FAQ

Questions from
Teams Like Yours

What's your typical engagement model?

+

We offer three models: project-based engagements with a defined scope, dedicated teams embedded into your org long-term, and staff augmentation for specific skill gaps. Most clients start with a project and expand to a dedicated team.

How long does a project typically take?

+

It depends on scope. A focused MVP can go from kick-off to launch in 8–12 weeks. Larger platform builds or long-term engagements run on ongoing sprint cycles. We move at startup speed — organized, but not slow.

Do you work with early-stage startups?

+

Yes. We've worked with companies from pre-seed through IPO. If you have a clear problem to solve and the right mindset, we can help — regardless of stage. Some of our best long-term relationships started with a single conversation and a rough idea.

How does your nearshore model work?

+

Our Guadalajara team operates in aligned US time zones — real-time collaboration, no async lag. They're senior engineers selected for both technical depth and cultural fit. Clients regularly say they can't tell the difference between our nearshore team and in-house staff.

What technologies do you specialize in?

+

iOS, Android, React Native, React, Node.js, Python, AWS, Azure, GCP — plus AI/ML integration, IoT, and FinTech-specific stacks. We choose the right technology for your problem, not the one we're most comfortable with.

What does the Initial Insights call look like?

+

It's a free 30-minute call with a senior Metova engineer — not a sales rep. We listen to your problem, ask the questions that matter, and give you honest feedback on approach, scope, and fit. No pitch deck, no pressure.
